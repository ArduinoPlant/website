Arduino Plant

We have presented the Arduino self watering plant as a project for the IMS science fair. Our aim is to present the Arduino plant project as an enterprise i.e. a complete business model from development to marketing and sales. For this purpose, we do not believe that just presenting a prototype model is enough. We also need to develop a website for marketing. Sales can be developed from the website through e-commerce. The website is easy to develop with some basic html code. It is also free to deploy on the github free webserver and only needs a username and password. For example, the time taken to develop and deploy this website was less than an hour.

As this project aims to turn ideas into an e-commerce business, the initial idea or prototype need not be an electronics or technical project. The initial idea could be anything e.g. handkercheif art or perfumes etc. Development of a website is a key landmark in turning ideas into financial income.
